about me

Hi there! My name is Bella Rivera, and I am a third-year computer science student at the University of Washington. Some of my favorite areas to work in are website development, product design and prototyping (as they relate to HCI), and of course full-stack software development. In addition, for the past couple of years, I have been involved in the teaching side of computer science, serving as both a teaching assistant and student lead for an introductory computer science program at the University of Washington.

On a more general note, I was born and raised in Seattle, Washington after both of my parents immigrated from the Philippines. Despite not being a huge fan of the weather, I love the city, which is why I chose that beautiful photo of downtown Seattle as my homepage background. Although I did not take the photo myself, I did some light editing on it, which is one of my favorite hobbies. I have such a passion for creative projects, whether that be photo/video editing, music production, sketching, writing poetry — the list goes on.

Other random interests: soccer, bubble tea, purple, karaoke, The Office, guitar, photography
